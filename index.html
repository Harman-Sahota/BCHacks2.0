What is BC Hacks 2.0 ? 


We're going digital! BC Hacks 2.0 is the second iteration of UBC Okanagan's own hackathon, organized by the Computer Science Course Union(CSCU), scheduled to take place anywhere with an Internet connection on February 20th - 21st, 2021! In 2020, we welcomed 164 hackers from all over the British Columbia for a 24-hour sprint where they built incredible projects, networked with prospective employers, and won fantastic prizes! 
   

                        About the CSCU


BC Hacks is organized by UBCO’s Computer Science Course Union. The current iteration of the CSCU was founded in 2018 by 3 students with a vision of an on-campus community for our fellow computer science students. Our goal is to improve the university experience for our peers the way we would want it improved. We do this by organizing hackathons, professional workshops, and collaborating with peer organizations at UBCO and beyond. 

Why BC Hacks 2.0 ? 

This year, we're going online, so you can participate from Kelowna, Canada, or anywhere around the globe! Whether you're a novice who hasn't written a line of code before, a tech expert who's built entire full stack applications, or your skillset is in an entirely different discipline, we want you with us! All we ask is you bring an interest in technology and a desire to expand your skillset. We'll make sure you have the tools and resources you need to be successful in whatever project you set your mind to! 

FAQ

A hackathon is best described as an “invention marathon”. Anyone who has an interest in technology attends a hackathon to learn, build & share their creations over the course of a weekend in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere.

BC Hacks 2.0 will begin @ 11am PST with our opening ceremony on February 20th and will conclude with project judging and our closing ceremony @ 1pm PST on February 22nd. Applications are due for hackers and mentors a week before the event on February 13th @ 11:59pm PST. More details about the event schedule will be sent to hackers via email after acceptance and will be posted to this website.

Not a dime! BC Hacks was, is, and always will be completely free for participants to attend.

High school students as well as university students from any faculty are permitted to attend! New graduates are also welcome to attend, however your graduation date must be within 1 year of February 20th, 2021. You don’t have to be a programmer and you certainly don’t have to be majoring in Computer Science. In fact, we encourage you to apply if you don't have a programming background because a lot more goes into a successful project than just the code ;)

All you need is a computer, an internet connection, and an open mind!
